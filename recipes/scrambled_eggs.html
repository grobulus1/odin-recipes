Scrambled eggs

An easy morning meal

One of the most popular yet good tasting dishes

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cooking oil
  • Green part of the onion
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Pre heat the pan
  2. Add eggs to the pan
  3. Add butter and milk
  4. Add salt
  5. Cook and put the dish on plate
  6. Dice green onion
  7. Put green onion on eggs
