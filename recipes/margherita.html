Margherita

An italian classic

Simple yet tasty dish

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato paste
  • Tomatoes
  • Cooking oil
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Mix water, flour and the egg
  2. Knead until u got a dough
  3. Roll the dough out
  4. Mix tomatoes with tomato paste to make sauce
  5. Add the sauce on to the dough
  6. Shred mozzarella cheese and add evenly
  7. Bake in an oven
